isInside ( Vector3  $v)

Checks whether the passed Vector3 is included in this Space.
Floating point vectors are accepted.

The contents of this function are based on Wolfram|MathWorld: Point-Lne Distance (3-Dimensional), whereas

X0 = $v, X1 = $this->baseCenter, X2 = $this->topCenter

.

Parameters
Vector3$vthe coordinates to check.
Returns
bool whether $v is inside the space.
